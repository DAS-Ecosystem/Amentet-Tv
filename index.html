About Krishnaye Kare Inc & Amethysts Of Amenti

Amentet TV is published under Krishnaye Kare Inc, a Netherlands-registered company (KVK 89977335) based in Amsterdam. Amethysts Of Amenti operates as a subdivision of Krishnaye Kare Inc, focused on immersive media and cultural-technology projects — blending cinema, XR platforms, and original storytelling rooted in mythology and spiritual heritage. Its projects span immersive film (including the flagship PadmaSambhava Calling), travel-documentary series, and interactive XR platforms. Amentet TV is one of the ways Krishnaye Kare Inc shares free, accessible content with a wider global audience. Learn more at amethystsofamenti.nl.

About the Platform

Amentet TV aggregates publicly available free streaming content from legal sources including iptv-org (open-source IPTV directory), YouTube official channels (VIZ Media, Toei Animation, university lecture channels, NASA), Tubi, and Pluto TV. All documentary and anime content links to official licensed platforms. No video files are hosted on this platform.

IPTV Disclaimer

IPTV channels are sourced from the open-source iptv-org/iptv repository, which indexes publicly available video stream URLs submitted by the community. Amentet TV does not host, store or distribute any video content. All streams are third-party public URLs. Channel availability is not guaranteed and may vary by region.
